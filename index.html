THE PROBLEM

Developing emotional intelligence from childhood is crucial, as it equips children with the skills to understand and manage their emotions, fostering healthier relationships and better decision-making. It also boosts self-esteem and resilience, contributing to a more harmonious society. When neglected in youth, emotional intelligence deficits can lead to long-term challenges in self-awareness and emotional control.

100% of the children have access to electronic devices*

40% of the children can't deal with their emotions in a healthy way*

20% of the children have difficulty socializing with peers*

*Data collected through an online survey

THE SOLUTION

To solve the above problem, we came up with Feel Fine: a mobile app to help children (ages 6-8) identify, understand and manage their emotions and feelings through many activities which will stimulate socioemotional development in a fun and interactive way.

Learning path

Learn the all the essentials with quick lessons. The lesson plan will be individualized for each child, according to their personal progress and organized with a spaced interval repetition technique.

Secondary Activities

Beyond the learning path, there're other activities to explore the large emotions' spectrum. Some activitied can be done whenever, and others are considered tasks and should be completed everyday.

Streak Reward System

To help the child stay motivated, we developed a streak system to reward the habit of self-improvement and learning. Complete the daily tasks for 7 days to get a reward. The rewards can be in-game or real-world items. Also, the tasks require only 15 minutes/day (less time on the phone).

Premium Store

Premium accounts can purchase real-world and some in-game items in the premium store. The coin used here is the XP obtained from lessons (the more you play, the more you gain). The child can purchase playable items to play with other people and strengthen their social skills while doing so.
